How to Use the Toy Tracker

Welcome Santa!

This tool helps you track and identify any discrepancies between toys made and toys shipped by your elves.

Features:

  • View each elf's toy production numbers
  • Click "Check for Naughty Elves" to compare toys made vs shipped
  • Red border and "NAUGHTY" banner will appear for elves with missing toys
  • Detailed breakdown of shipped toys will appear under each elf

How it works: The system recursively checks all regional warehouses and shipping locations to ensure no toys are unaccounted for.
