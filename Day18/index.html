How to Use the Christmas Budget Analyser 3000

  • Set your Christmas budget using the budget input at the top
  • View your current shopping list and filter between all items, Christmas gifts, or regular items
  • Track your spending with the progress bar - it will warn you if you go over budget
  • Add new items using the form at the bottom
  • Christmas gifts are highlighted in pink and marked with a 🎁
  • Regular shopping items are marked with a 🛍️
  • Use the 'Make Gift/Make Regular' button to change an item's category
  • Remove items using the trash can button 🗑️
