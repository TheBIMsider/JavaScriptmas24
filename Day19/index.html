How to Use This App

Adding Items

1. Choose an emoji (optional):

  • Click the emoji box to select from our collection
  • Use your emoji keyboard (Windows: Win + . | Mac: Cmd + Ctrl + Space)
  • If no emoji is selected, a gift emoji üéÅ will be used

2. Enter the item name and price (required)

3. Click "Add Item" to add to your list

Managing Your List

You can:

  • Sort items by price (low to high or high to low)
  • Sort items alphabetically (A to Z or Z to A)
  • Shuffle items randomly
  • Remove individual items with the "Remove" button
