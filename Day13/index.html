How It Works

Santa is charging $5 per delivery this year! Here's how to use the calculator:

  • Enter payment amounts separated by commas (e.g., 5,10,20)
  • Santa only accepts $5, $10, and $20 bills
  • The calculator will check if Santa can give correct change to everyone
  • A happy present means everyone got their change!
  • A sad present means someone didn't get correct change
