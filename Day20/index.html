How it Works

Welcome to the Santa's Workshop Elf-ID Maker! Here's how to use it:

  1. Enter your first and last name (required)
  2. Optionally add a middle name for an extra magical touch
  3. Click "Generate Elf Name" to receive your unique elf identity

Each generated name:

  • Matches your initials
  • Is unique to you
  • Gets added to the "Registered Employees" list

Perfect for holiday events, Secret Santa, or bringing some Christmas cheer to the workplace!
